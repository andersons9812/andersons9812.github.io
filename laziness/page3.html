Treatments

If you want to stop being lazy and actually get something productive done, here are some options.

  • Put away technology
  • Stretch
  • Get off the couch
  • Take a 30 minute nap to gain some energy back
  • Drink some coffee or tea.
  • Splash some water in your face.
  • Improve your diet
  • Clean up your workspace
Specific Laziness Symptom Treatment
Laying on the couch Get off the couch... duh
No motivation Drink a coffee, give yourself a pep talk, and get to work!
Tired Drink a coffee, or have someone throw cold water into your face.
Can't reach the remote from the couch Call your little brother in the room to get it for you. (oh wait... I guess that's encouraging laziness, oh well)

*And if you still want to be lazy, then be lazy and smart about it. Studies show that laziness improves one's smartness and innovative side so it's not neccessarily a bad thing!*
