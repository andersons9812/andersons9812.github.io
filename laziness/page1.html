Causes

There are many factors that cause laziness

  • Work/Homework: fatigue from work or the thought of having work to do or homework to do.
  • School: Going to school all day
  • Items out of reach: Cannot reach items from a couch or bed
  • Tiring Activities: Tired out from exerting energy.
  • Anything that takes effort
  • Too many more causes to list
