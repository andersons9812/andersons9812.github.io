Introduction to Laziness

Laziness is the quality of being unwilling to exert energy or work. Most people demonstrate laziness throughout their lives.Some people associate being lazy with being a couch potato and the word "lazy" often as a negative connotation. However, being lazy can also make one smarter and more innovative with the creation of ideas that make an activity more convenient.
